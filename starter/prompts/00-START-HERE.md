# Start here — your own estimator in about 15 minutes

This kit becomes **your** estimating app. You don't write any code — Claude does the editing; you answer questions.

## Get set up with Claude (one time)

1. **Download this kit:** on the [DRYscope GitHub page](https://github.com/kcarlson-dm/dryscope), click the green **Code** button → **Download ZIP** → unzip it. The kit is the `starter` folder inside.
2. **Open Claude** (the claude.ai desktop app) and start a **Cowork** session — when it asks which folder to work in, **pick your unzipped `starter` folder**. That's the whole trick: Claude can now read and edit the kit's files directly.
3. Run the prompt files below **in order**. Open each one, copy everything under *"Paste this to Claude,"* paste it into Claude, and answer its questions.

## The steps

1. **`01-company-and-tax.md`** — your company identity + your sales-tax areas. *(Everyone does this — tax is required to calculate an estimate, no matter how you price.)*
2. **`02-build-your-pricing.md`** — Claude asks you to choose: **enter your own rates manually**, or **derive them with ODAPM**, the free open pricing model at [getodapm.github.io](https://getodapm.github.io).
3. **`03-deploy-online.md`** — *optional* — put your estimator on a free web link your whole crew can open from any phone.

## When you're done

Open `index.html` in a browser and build a test estimate. Your company name should be on it, your tax areas in the dropdown, and no line should price at $0 unless you left it unpriced on purpose.
